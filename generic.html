Vertex Pharmaceuticals
Financial Dashboard

Explore Vertex Pharma's dynamic dashboard showcasing comprehensive analytics
on sales performance, customer insights, and market trends.

Crafting the financial dashboard for Vertex Pharma involved a careful selection of essential metrics, including revenue, gross profit, operating profit, ROA, OPEX, current ratio, cash ratio, and asset turnover. By incorporating these metrics and enabling data validation through a user-friendly dropdown menu for selecting different years, I aimed to provide a comprehensive view of the company's financial health and performance trends. Through this intuitive interface, users can easily analyze historical data, identify patterns, and make informed decisions.

Moreover, the dashboard offers detailed insights into revenue generated from various medications, alongside comparisons of % growth between the current and previous years. This granular analysis of product performance facilitates a deeper understanding of market dynamics and assists in strategic planning. Ultimately, the financial dashboard equips Vertex Pharma with the tools needed to optimize operations, allocate resources effectively, and drive sustainable growth in the competitive pharmaceutical landscape.
